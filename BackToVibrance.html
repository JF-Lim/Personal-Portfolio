Explorers

It is never too late to explore

the vibrancy of colours galore.

Listen to the sweet symphonies

of faraway lands inspiring epiphanies

Towards the floating castle of stardust

to fulfil our whimsical wanderlust.

Chilly Luxury

Sweet auburn autumn

did not feel so cold as I snuggle

in my chilly luxury.

The maple leaves

said their goodbyes

as they rest for the winter.

Slowly, I fell into a warm slumber.
