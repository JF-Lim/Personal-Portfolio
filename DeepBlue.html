Blazing Cosmos

The blazing cosmos

sears through the past.

The silhouettes

gazed in silent awe.

Reminiscent of

the transient spark of life,

death soon robbed the stars of their vitality.

The Vain Light

The light like no other

shines with dull lustre.

Yet in the haunted street,

the light had no one to greet.

In bitter anguish, the light cried out:

"How redundant of an existence!

For no one appreciates my brillance!"

In that moment, the light lost its spark

and the night soon turned dark.
